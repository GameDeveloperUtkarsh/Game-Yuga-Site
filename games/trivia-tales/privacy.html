Privacy Policy for Trivia Tales

Effective Date: 12 March 2024

Developer: Game Yuga

1. Introduction

Welcome to Trivia Tales, a mobile quiz game developed by Game Yuga. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you use the Trivia Tales application (“App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information Collected

a. In-Game Username: We collect the username you choose within the game. Usernames are stored in Firebase only if your score qualifies for the leaderboard. Others are not retained.

b. Reported Questions: When you report a trivia question, we collect relevant details to improve question quality and accuracy.

3. Data Storage and Processing

a. Firebase Database: Firebase stores leaderboard usernames and reported questions securely, adhering to industry standards.

b. Local Device Storage: Your game progress (e.g., levels, scores) is stored locally on your device and is not accessed or stored by us.

4. Data Retention

Leaderboard usernames are stored only while they remain in the rankings. Reported questions are reviewed and cleared daily after action is taken.

5. Age Requirements

Trivia Tales is intended for users aged 9 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from users below this age.

6. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, modify, or delete your data. Please contact us at the email below to exercise your rights.

7. Security Measures

We employ reasonable safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

8. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase, which operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage users to review it here: Firebase Privacy Policy

The App may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted within the App with an updated effective date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact us at:
