Privacy Policy for Way Out

Effective Date: [April 4, 2025]

Developer: Game Yuga

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of Way Out. This includes, but is not limited to, names, email addresses, location data, or device identifiers.

2. AdMob Advertising

Way Out displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising network. While we do not collect data ourselves, AdMob may collect and use certain information (such as device identifiers and usage data) to serve contextual or interest-based ads, in accordance with their own privacy practices.

Because Way Out is directed at children under the age of 13, we have configured AdMob to serve only child-appropriate, non-personalized ads, in compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and relevant Google Play policies.

For more information on how AdMob handles user data, please refer to: Google Privacy Policy

3. Children’s Privacy

Way Out is designed for a general audience and may appeal to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. In compliance with COPPA, we have taken steps to ensure that any third-party services used in the game do not collect personal information from children either.

If a parent or guardian believes that we have inadvertently collected personal data from their child, they may contact us at game.yuga.studio@gmail.com, and we will promptly take steps to remove such information.

4. Third-Party Links

Way Out does not contain direct links to external websites or social media platforms. All ads shown are managed through AdMob and comply with their child-safe advertising standards.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or made available via the app store listing. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
